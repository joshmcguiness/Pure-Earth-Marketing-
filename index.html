AI & LLM Marketing Opportunities

New AI-powered search and advertising channels Pure Earth should be aware of and preparing for

CRITICAL

Google AI Overviews (SGE)

Google now shows AI-generated summaries at the top of search results. Being referenced in these overviews is the new Page 1. Pure Earth needs structured data, expert content, and authority signals to be cited.

Action: Add FAQ schema markup to all product pages. Create expert-level content about Kakadu Plum and aluminium packaging that AI can cite.
Cost: $300–$800 for schema implementation + content
NEW CHANNEL

Perplexity AI Advertising

Perplexity launched sponsored results in AI search. Early adopters are getting cheap CPCs ($0.30–$0.80) on beauty and wellness queries. This is where Google Ads was 10 years ago.

Action: Set up a Perplexity Ads account and run test campaigns on "natural shampoo" and "sustainable beauty" queries. First-mover advantage is significant.
Cost: $200–$500/mo test budget for early learning
OPPORTUNITY

ChatGPT Product Recommendations

When users ask ChatGPT "what’s the best natural shampoo in Australia?", it recommends brands with strong web presence, reviews, and structured data. Pure Earth is currently NOT recommended.

Action: Build web presence that LLMs can reference — expert blog posts, PR coverage, and third-party reviews. Get listed on comparison and review sites.
Cost: $500–$1,500 for content + review outreach
OPPORTUNITY

GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation)

A new discipline focused on getting brands cited by AI assistants (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity). Requires authoritative, factual, well-structured content that AI models prefer to reference.

Action: Create a "Pure Earth Facts" page with clear claims, statistics, certifications, and ingredient data. Structure for AI readability.
Cost: $200–$600 for structured content creation
NEW CHANNEL

Meta AI & TikTok AI Ad Targeting

Both Meta and TikTok now offer AI-powered ad targeting (Advantage+ and Smart Performance). These use machine learning to find the highest-converting audiences automatically, outperforming manual targeting by 20–40%.

Action: Switch current ad campaigns to Advantage+ (Meta) and Smart Performance (TikTok). Upload product catalogue for dynamic creative.
Cost: Same ad budget, just better targeting. $0 extra for setup.
CRITICAL

Amazon AU & AI Shopping

Amazon’s Rufus AI shopping assistant now recommends products to millions of Australian shoppers. Not being on Amazon AU means missing AI-driven product discovery entirely.

Action: List at least 2–3 hero products on Amazon AU. Optimise listings for Rufus AI with detailed ingredients, benefits, and comparison keywords.
Cost: $500–$1,000 setup + Amazon referral fees (15%)

Scoring Formulas

Engagement Rate

(Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves) / Views x 100

Target: >5% (TikTok), >3% (Instagram), >2% (LinkedIn)

Virality Score (/100)

Shares (x3) + Saves (x2) + Comments (x1.5) + Likes (x1) / Normalised to 100

Weights shares and saves highest as these signal strongest content resonance.

Content Efficiency Ratio

Engagement Value / Production Time (hours)

Prioritise formats that generate highest returns per hour invested.

Platform Health Score

Follower Growth Rate + Avg Eng. Rate + Reach Growth / 3

Weekly trend indicator to spot platform momentum or decline.
